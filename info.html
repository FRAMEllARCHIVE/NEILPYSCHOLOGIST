My Approach

I am solution-focused and practice a range of psychodynamic therapies, mindfulness approaches, and behavioral interventions. I also specialize in BWRT (Brain Working Recursive Therapy), which is highly effective in treating a wide range of mental health conditions.

Ketamine-Assisted Psychotherapy

Ketamine-assisted psychotherapy (KAP) involves prescribed doses of ketamine used as an adjunct to psychotherapy. KAP is effective for treating conditions like treatment-resistant depression (TRD), anxiety, OCD, PTSD, and substance abuse.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Therapy and Counselling?

Therapy is simply talking to someone within a safe, confidential space. It allows you to open up about your feelings, challenges, and express yourself freely. A skilled therapist helps you make sense of your experiences and deepens your self-awareness to enhance your coping skills.

Does therapy or counselling help?

Yes! Therapy is evidence-based and helps treat anxiety, depression, addiction, and other emotional challenges. It can lead to improved self-awareness and better coping mechanisms.

How do I know if I need counselling?

If you are struggling with emotional challenges such as difficulty focusing, sleeping, stress, or feeling disconnected from life, therapy can help. These are common signs that therapy can support you in making positive changes.

How many sessions will I need?

Sessions typically range from 3 to 6, depending on your circumstances. The first session helps determine a plan for your treatment. More sessions may be needed based on progress.

How much does it cost?
